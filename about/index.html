About me

Brian Kroll is a technologist from Chicago, Illinois. He is the co-founder and former CFO of The Lucy Parsons Labs, a former organizer of Cryptoparty Chicago, and Technoactivism 3rd Mondays Chicago, a privacy and digital rights advocate, an activist, a unionist, spelunker of networks, explorer of cities, cryptography enthiuist, cybernetic steerer, radio listener, project manager, data lover, and photographer.
